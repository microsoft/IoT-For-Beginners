# Research sensors and actuators

## Instructions

Dis lesson talk about sensors and actuators. Make research and describe one sensor and one actuator wey fit work with IoT dev kit, include:

* Wetin e dey do
* Di electronics/hardware wey dey inside
* E be analog or digital
* Wetin be di units and range of inputs or measurements
