# Investigate IoT project

## Instructions

Plenty big and small IoT project dey happen for di world, from smart farm to smart city, for healthcare monitoring, transport, and how people dey use public space.

Search internet for one project wey you like, e good make e dey near where you dey live. Talk about di good side and di bad side of di project, like wetin e dey benefit, any wahala wey e dey cause and how dem dey take care of privacy.

## Rubric

| Criteria | Exemplary | Adequate | Needs Improvement |
| -------- | --------- | -------- | ----------------- |
| Explain di good side and bad side | Explain di good side and bad side of di project well well | Explain di good side and bad side small | No explain di good side or di bad side |
