# Train your classifier for plenty fruits and vegetables

## Instructions

For dis lesson, you don train image classifier wey fit sabi di difference between ripe and unripe fruits, but na only one type of fruit you use. You fit train classifier wey go sabi plenty fruits, but how e go work well go depend on di type of fruit and di difference between ripe and unripe.

For example, fruits wey dey change color when dem ripe, image classifier fit no work well reach color sensor because dem dey use grey scale images instead of full color.

Try train your classifier with other fruits make you see how e go work, especially when di fruits resemble each other. For example, apple and tomato.
