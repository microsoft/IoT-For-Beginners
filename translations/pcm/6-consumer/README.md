# Consumer IoT - build smart voice assistant

Di food don grow, dem don carry am go processing plant, sort am for quality, sell am for store, and now na time to cook! One important thing wey dey any kitchen na timer. For di beginning, dem start with hour glass - your food go don cook when all di sand don waka go di bottom bulb. Later, dem change am to clockwork, then electric.

Di latest version now na part of our smart devices. For kitchens wey dey houses all over di world, you go hear cooks dey shout "Hey Siri - set 10 minute timer", or "Alexa - cancel my bread timer". You no need waka go back kitchen to check timer again, you fit do am from your phone, or just shout across di room.

For dis 4 lessons, you go learn how to build smart timer, use AI to sabi your voice, understand wetin you dey ask, and reply you with info about your timer. You go also add support for plenty languages.
