# Configure your microphone and speakers - Virtual IoT Hardware

Di virtual IoT hardware go use microphone and speakers wey dey attach to your computer.

If your computer no get microphone and speakers wey dem build inside, you go need attach dem using hardware wey you like, like:

* USB microphone  
* USB speakers  
* Speakers wey dem build inside your monitor and connect am with HDMI  
* Bluetooth headset  

Check di instructions wey your hardware manufacturer give to install and configure di hardware.
